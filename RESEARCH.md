# Research Notes — Hoppers Science Consultants

## Confirmed Science Consultant

### Dr. Emily Fairfax
- **Role**: Science Expert (credited in film)
- **Title**: Assistant Professor of Geography, University of Minnesota
- **Department**: Geography, Environment, and Society
- **Affiliation**: Saint Anthony Falls Laboratory, University of Minnesota
- **Email**: efairfax@umn.edu
- **Website**: https://emilyfairfaxscience.com/
- **LinkedIn**: https://www.linkedin.com/in/emilyfairfax/
- **Education**: Carleton College '14
- **Specialization**: Ecohydrology, beaver ecosystem engineering, wildfire/drought resilience

### Contribution to Hoppers
- Spent 5 years consulting with Pixar animation team
- Took Pixar's art team into the field for a week to observe real beaver ponds
- Provided technical beaver ecology talks and reviewed scientific accuracy throughout production
- Character **Dr. Samantha E. Fairfax** (voiced by Kathy Najimy) named in her honor
- Credited as "Science Expert" in closing credits

### Key Research Areas (Relevant to Lessons)
- How beavers create drought-resistant and fire-resistant patches in landscapes
- Beaver ecosystem engineering and its effects on water flow, biodiversity, and habitat
- Ecohydrology — the intersection of ecology and hydrology
